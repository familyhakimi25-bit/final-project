Got questions?

Quick answers about bookings, reschedules, payments, and more.

How do I make a booking?
Go to Home and click Book Now. Choose date, time, and your preferred barber, then confirm.
Can I reschedule or cancel?
Yes. Use your confirmation link to reschedule up to 2 hours before your slot.
Do you accept walk-ins?
Limited walk-ins are accepted; online bookings have priority.
What payment methods are supported?
Cash, cards, and popular e-wallets at the shop.
