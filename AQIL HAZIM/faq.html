Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about bookings, payments and the barbershop.

How do I make a booking?

Go to the Home page and click Book Now. Choose your date, time and barber, then confirm.

Can I reschedule or cancel?

Yes. Use your confirmation link to reschedule up to 2 hours before your slot.

Do you accept walk-ins?

Limited walk-ins are accepted, but bookings get priority.

What payment methods are supported?

We accept cash, card, and popular e-wallets at the shop.
